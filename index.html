Creations

Creations is an Architectural firm established in the year 1998 in Guwahati, Assam. Ever since its inception, it has been engaged in the design & development of high performance & sustainable architecture. Its design domain comprises of master plans, individual buildings & components of these. So far it has been able to cater to a clientele of the entire North-Eastern part of the country. The primary use of these designs are civic, commercial, cultural, hospitality, residential & mixed use.
